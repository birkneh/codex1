3-Day Gym Checklist + Load Tracker

Goal: fat loss + muscle gain, while reducing heavy deadlift/squat loading until glute pain settles.

Week Setup

Enter body weight and press Calculate Protein.
Pain rule: keep glute pain at or below 3/10 during training. If pain shoots down leg, causes numbness/weakness, or worsens over time, stop and get a clinician assessment.

Program Notes

Schedule: Day 1 (Mon), Day 2 (Wed), Day 3 (Fri) or any non-consecutive pattern.

Progression: when you hit top reps for all sets, increase load 2.5-5% next week.

Intensity: leave 1-2 reps in reserve on most sets.

Warm-up: 10-15 min cardio at the start, plus 5 min stretching at the end.
